## How to Use
The spreadsheet is color-coded for ease of use:

### 1. Inputs (Yellow Cells üü®)
*   **Warehouse Dimensions:** Enter your Length, Width, and Height (m).
*   **Operational Constraints:** Enter your Usable Space Factor (default is 0.75).
*   **Inventory Data:** Enter current pallet counts and average pallet dimensions.
*   **Rack Configuration:** Define your bays, levels, and depth.

### 2. Outputs (Green Cells üü©)
*   **Gross vs. Usable Volume:** The tool automatically strips out aisle space.
*   **Inventory Cube:** Calculates total volume occupied by stock.
*   **Cubic Utilization %:** The strategic "Air Check."
*   **Pallet Utilization %:** The operational "Slot Check."
